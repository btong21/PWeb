A New App

Co-founder | May 2012 - September 2012 | Hong Kong

Pain, Problem

Digital entertainers are always on the lookout to discover new channels to deliver their content to their fans. Most wanted to ride the mobile wave as their fans entered a new era of computing. But in order to create a mobile app that tailors to an entertainers’ needs in this digital age, the reliance of technical knowledge is needed.

Solution, Value Proposition

With A New App, entertainers can create their own personalized mobile applications without the knowledge of code (or a programmer friend). The easy drag and drop interface allows app creators to connect existing digital content (YouTube, Twitter, SoundCloud, Vimeo) while customising their own user interface in 3 easy steps.

Grapevine

Co-founder | June 2013 - May 2014 | Vancouver

Pain, Problem

Although word of mouth advertising is the most effective way to attract new loyal customers, small businesses cannot find ways to track their brand advocates’ influence.

Solution, Value Proposition

Grapevine gives business owners the ability to send their most loyal customers “social vouchers”. They are shareable, trackable SMS or email deals sent directly to their customers’ smartphones. With these “social vouchers", businesses can collect quantifiable data on their top brand advocates and their influence as their friends redeem the vouchers.
